Pricing questions
Is the free plan really free forever?
+
Yes — no time limit, no credit card, no catch. The Free plan gives you real, useful finance tracking tools that work indefinitely. We may add more features to paid plans over time, but we'll never remove features from Free or move them behind a paywall.
What happens after my 14-day trial?
+
After the trial your Plus or Pro plan continues and you're billed for the first time. If you decide it's not for you, cancel before the trial ends — no charge, no questions asked. Your data is never deleted; you simply drop back to the Free plan limits.
Can I switch plans or cancel anytime?
+
Yes, always. Upgrade or downgrade from your account settings at any time. Cancelling a paid plan takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you keep access until then. If you cancel an annual plan early, we'll refund any unused full months.
What payment methods do you accept?
+
We accept Visa, Mastercard, and Airtel Money / MTN Mobile Money for Zambian users. All payments are processed by a PCI-compliant provider — we never store your card details on our servers.
Are prices in Zambian Kwacha?
+
Yes — prices shown are in ZMW (Zambian Kwacha) by default. International users can select their currency during checkout. Exchange rates are applied at the time of payment using live conversion rates.
Do you offer discounts for students or NGOs?
+
Yes! We offer a 50% discount for verified students and registered non-profit organisations. Contact us with proof of eligibility and we'll set you up.
