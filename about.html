Our story

Built because
spreadsheets are terrible and complicated

Folo started as a side project by a student who had 11 tabs open trying to figure out where their pocket money went.There had to be a better way.

The problem we kept running into:

Most finance apps are either too simple (a notes app with totals) or too complex (built for accountants, not regular people). We wanted something that felt like talking to a smart friend who happens to know a lot about money - clear, honest, and not overwhelming.

The solution:

we built Folo: a personal finance tracker that respects your time, protects your data, and actually helps you understand your financial picture.

What we believe about money

Financial clarity shouldn't be a privilege. Whether you're managing K500 a month or K20,000, you deserve to know where your money goes and have a plan for where it should go next.

Folo is designed for everyday people — not just high earners or finance enthusiasts. We keep it simple, honest, and genuinely useful.

Where we're headed

We're a small team with a clear mission: help people feel in control of their money. We're building Folo one thoughtful feature at a time — starting with the things that actually move the needle.

No bloat. No dark patterns. No selling your data. Just a tool that works for you.

What guides us

Our principles

01

Clarity over clutter

Every screen, every feature, every word is chosen to make your financial picture clearer — not to impress you with complexity.

02

Your data belongs to you

We never sell, share, or monetize your financial data.You can export or delete everything, anytime.

03

Honest by design

No dark patterns, no hidden fees, no nudging you toward premium features you don't need. What you see is what you get.

04

Built to last

We're not chasing growth metrics. We're building something sustainable — a tool people can rely on for decades, not months.

Security & privacy

Your money data is safe with us

We know that financial information is deeply personal. We've built Folo from the ground up with security and privacy as non-negotiable foundations — not afterthoughts.

All data encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256 and TLS 1.3
We never sell, rent, or share your personal or financial data with third parties
Two-factor authentication available on all accounts
Full data export and permanent account deletion available at any time
Regular independent security audits and penetration testing
