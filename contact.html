Common questions
Is Folo really free to use?
+
Yes — Folo's core features are free. We may offer optional paid features in the future, but we'll always maintain a solid free tier and never surprise you with paywalls.
Can I export my data?
+
Absolutely. You can export all your transactions and account data as CSV at any time from your account settings. Your data is always yours.
How do I report a bug?
+
Use the form above and select "Bug report" as the topic. Include what you were doing, which browser you were using, and any error messages. We take bugs seriously and try to fix them fast.
How do I delete my account?
+
You can permanently delete your account and all associated data from Settings → Account → Delete account. The deletion is immediate and irreversible. We'll send a confirmation to your email.
